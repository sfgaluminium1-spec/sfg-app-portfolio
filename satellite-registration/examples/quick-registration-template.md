## Tips

1. **Be specific** - Provide clear descriptions for each section
2. **Use examples** - Show real workflows and use cases
3. **Document integrations** - List all external systems you connect to
4. **Define business rules** - Explain the logic and constraints
5. **List all capabilities** - Don't hold back, document everything your app can do
6. **Include API details** - Help other apps integrate with yours
